Must Watch Movies!!

Kaylee's top 3 movies

Spirited Away

I love Studio Ghibli movies. Spirited Away feels like a fever dream, and I mean that in a good way. It is one of those movies that I can rewatch and with each rewatch, I think I enjoy it more and more. The music is amazing and nostalgic. The art is beautiful. yes.

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Oh man... this movie. It's super cool. This movie portrays how a person's life could turn out differently from making different choices and happenings.. There is also the theory of multitudes of universes or versions of yourself. I laughed and cried watching this. This movie reminds you to be appreciative for the life you have and the people in it.

Crazy Rich Asians

This movie is not necessarily a "top 3," but I can't think of another movie to talk about. This movie has good laughs, sad sads, and sweet moments.
